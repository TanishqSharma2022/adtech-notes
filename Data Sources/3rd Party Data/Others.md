### Numerator 
The main consumer app is called Receipt Hog, which allows users to earn rewards for sharing their shopping data. Users take pictures of their shopping receipts from any retailer to earn points. 

![[Pasted image 20260204102901.png]]

Note that Numerator's data cannot be used for specific user targetting but for integrating with other datasets and getting insights from them.

This is transactional data that is used in COMMERCE section in MIQ SIGMA.
